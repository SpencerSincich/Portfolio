Example for quick start

Note: The estimated time required to complete this procedure is 30 minutes.

A construction team is building a house.

The client requests a quote for adding a kitchen to the patio area.

For this example, the app will enable the team to track the quote.
The project manager wants to create a:
  • Project.
  • List to contain the quote.
  • Task to describe the quote.
 
 

(Step 1) - Create a project

To open the demo app, click this link:
https://demo--teamtasks.bubbleapps.io/
  1. Near the top of the left-pane, click the "New Project" button.
    The "Add project" window will appear.
  2. In the first field, create a name for the project. For this example: "23 Bird Ave".
  3. Click the "CREATE" button.
    You will see the project name appear in the:
    • Header.
    • Left-pane.

(Step 2) - Create a list

  1. In the top-right corner of the page's white section, click the "New List" button.
    The "Add list" window will appear.
  2. For this example, type the word "Quotes" in the "List name" field.
  3. Click the "CREATE" button.
    You will see the list appear in the page's white section.

(Step 3) - Create a task

  1. Under the list's name, click the "New Task" button.
    The "Add task" window will appear.
  2. In the "Task name" field, write the name for the quote. For this example: "Add kitchen to patio area".
  3. For this article, ignore the next 2 headings.
  4. Under the "Start date" heading, click to select today's date.
  5. Under the "End date" heading, click to select tomorrow's date.
  6. For now, ignore the "Category" heading, because "Preset" is the only available category.
  7. Under the "Status" heading, you will see the 3 options describing the completion status of the work described in the quote.
    Since the work hasn't started yet, click to select "Planned".
  8. Under the "% Complete" heading, you will see the field enabled for numbers from 0-100. 
    For this example, type-in the number: "34".
    Note: After you save the task, you will see the percentage displayed in the task's progress bar.
  9. On the list of team members, click the square on the row for each team member who will be involved in this task.
    Note: The team members you select here will each see their name when viewing the task's row in the list. 
  10. Click the "CREATE" button.
    You will see the task's row appear in the list.
 

What tasks will your team plan and track?

For example:
  • Work on-site.
  • Paperwork.
  • Changes to the contract.
